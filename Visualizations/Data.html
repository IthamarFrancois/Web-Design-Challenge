HUMIDITY


Generally speaking, this dataset shows that as you go further north (right/positive latitude), the humidity in those cities are getting higher (concentrated between 60%-100%) as opposed to the number of cities further south (left/negative latitude). Also, very rarely do you get a city with 0% humidity, happening only closer to the North Pole. Very low humidity % is generally rare.
